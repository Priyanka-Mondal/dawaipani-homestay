Dining

Home-Cooked Mountain Meals

Our resident chef brings the warmth of a home kitchen to every meal. Using fresh local produce and traditional recipes of the hills, expect hearty thalis, steaming bowls of thukpa, handmade momos, and vegetables sourced from the surrounding hillside gardens.

Meals are served in our open dining area, where the Kangchenjunga range sits on the horizon like a painting. There is no better backdrop for breakfast than those snow-capped peaks glowing in the morning light — a view you will carry with you long after you leave.
