⚙️  Owner Setup Guide — Enable Email Notifications

To receive booking emails and automatically send confirmations to guests, complete these one-time steps:

  1. Go to emailjs.com and create a free account (free tier: 200 emails / month — enough for a small homestay).
  2. Click Add New Service → choose Gmail → connect your Google account (placeholder@gmail.com). Note your Service ID.
  3. Go to Email Templates → Create Template.
    Make Template 1 (booking alert for you, the owner):
      Subject: New Booking Request — {{guest_name}}
      To email: {{to_email}}
      Body: include {{guest_name}}, {{guest_email}}, {{guest_phone}}, {{check_in}}, {{check_out}}, {{nights}}, {{guests}}, {{rooms}}, {{message}}.
  4. Make Template 2 (confirmation for your guest):
      Subject: Your Booking Request — Dawaipani Homestay
      To email: {{to_email}}
      Body example: "Dear {{guest_name}}, thank you for your request from {{check_in}} to {{check_out}}. We have received your booking request and the owner will contact you within 24 hours to confirm your stay. — Dawaipani Homestay"
  5. Go to Account → API Keys → copy your Public Key.
  6. Open js/booking.js and replace the four placeholder values at the top of the file (YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY, YOUR_SERVICE_ID, YOUR_OWNER_TEMPLATE_ID, YOUR_GUEST_TEMPLATE_ID).
  7. To update availability: edit data/bookings.json — add date ranges to fullyBooked or partiallyBooked arrays, then commit and push to GitHub. The calendar updates automatically.
